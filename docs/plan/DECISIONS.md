# Architecture & Design Decisions

Key decisions made during planning and development. Use these to guide implementation and prevent revisiting settled questions.

---

## Decision Status Legend

- `pending` — Under discussion, not yet decided
- `accepted` — Decided and in effect
- `rejected` — Considered and rejected (keep for context)
- `superseded` — Replaced by a newer decision
